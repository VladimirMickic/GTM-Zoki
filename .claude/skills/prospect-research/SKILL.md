name: prospect-research
description: Research a prospect company and produce a campaign-ready brief. Use when preparing for outbound, building target lists, or onboarding a new client.
allowed-tools: Read, Write, WebSearch, WebFetch
---

## Phase 1: Gather
1. Search for company website, recent news, leadership
2. Identify industry, size, tech stack if visible
3. Find relevant pain points from ICP.md

## Phase 2: Analyze
1. Match company against ICP criteria
2. Identify buying triggers (hiring, funding, expansion)
3. Find the right contact title from ICP buyer profile

## Phase 3: Output Brief
Output a structured markdown brief:
- Company overview (2-3 sentences)
- ICP fit score (Strong / Moderate / Weak)
- Pain points (mapped to their situation)
- Buying triggers identified
- Recommended angle for outreach
- Suggested contact titles
